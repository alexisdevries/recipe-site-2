Chicken Kebabs

This recipe was adapted from the recipe on the Website Once Upon a Chef and written by Jenn Segal

Ingredents

  • 2 pounds of boneless skinless chicken thighs cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons of paprika
  • 0.5 teaspoons of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • the Zest from one lemon
  • the juice from one lemon
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 0.5 teaspoons of cracked black pepper
  • 5 Garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 large red onion cut into 1 inch chunks
  • vegetable oil for greasing the grill

Directions

  1. In a bowl, combine the yougurt, olive oil, paprika, cumin, red pepper flakes, lemon zest, lemon juice, salt, pepper and garlic.
  2. Start skewering on your chicken pieces alternating with your pieces of onion place the prepared kebabs on your baking sheet
  3. On the baking sheet lather your chicken skewers with your yougurt marinade with the silicone brush
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat lightly grease your grill grates. Place your kebabs on the grill for about 13-15 minutes intermittently turning them.
  5. Once finished remove the kebabs from the grill and serve
